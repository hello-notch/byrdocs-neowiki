## Question 9

$x_1=\frac12$, $x_{n+1}=\frac1{2+x_n}$.

### 1.

If $\{x_n\}$ is convergent, find $\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_n$.

<Solution>$\lim_{n \to \infty} x_n = \sqrt{2} - 1$</Solution>

### 2.

Prove $\{x_{2n+1}\}$ is convergent.

<Solution>
$\{x_{2n+1}\}$ 单调有界，故收敛。
</Solution>

### 3.

Prove $\{x_n\}$ is convergent.

<Solution>

</Solution>
